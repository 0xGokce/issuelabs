Our Story
There is a problem in our society. The standardised feeling of clothing, manufactured relationships with brands and consumerist mindset within communities. As we innovated to get closer to simplicity, ease and accesibility we got further from authenticity, uniqueness and value. When did you value what you wear, the story it bears, the last time? What we wear is a statement. As a cross-discipline sustainable innovation platfrom, The Issue Labs is an open-source platform and community to lead the change for circular mindsets. The issue is a movement of re-defining perfection with imperfection. We are starting with fashion.
